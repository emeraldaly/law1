Startup Weekend

Project: Unbound Art Demo Site from Weekend

At Startup Weekend, people pitch ideas on Friday, form teams, and work to present their business concept by Sunday. This site is the minimum viable product that accompanied Unbound Art's presentation. The site was a demo of the process-- a site where artists upload their work via Instagram and buyers can rent or buy pieces. The team won first place! Alyson was the developer.

This was Alyson's third Startup Weekend and the first after receiving formal coding training. She previously worked on teams in DC and Paris as a designer and non-tech business role.

Project: Unbound Art current Landing Page

The Unbound Art team has continued to work together further refining the idea through several phases of market validation. This is the current site, which at this point is mainly to collect emails for updates as the business continues to evolve.

Good-Form

Project: An administration and communication site for educational organizations

Good Form is a web application that streamlines administration and communication for educational organizations. It allows administrators to register and track students and classes, as well as other administrative metrics, including attendance, assignments, and lecture details.

It features a separate interface hub for students, where important dates and announcements may be seen and assignments submitted. All users of Good Form can connect with other users by looking up profile details.
